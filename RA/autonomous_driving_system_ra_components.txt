ML_AI_DL_Algorithms: Provide intelligent perception, prediction, and decision-making capabilities. They process collected data to enable automation and adaptive behavior.

Data_Collection: Gathers data from sensors, internal networks, and external sources. It prepares structured inputs for analytics and control functions.

UI_UX_and_Infotainment: Delivers user interaction and multimedia functionalities. It enables visualization, configuration, and user engagement with the system.

Real_time_and_Critical_Control_Software: Executes time-sensitive and safety-critical functions. It ensures deterministic behavior and reliable system operation.

Software_Frameworks_and_Standards: Provide middleware and standardized interfaces for application development. They manage communication, scheduling, and hardware abstraction.

Internal_Networking_Interfaces: Enable data exchange between hardware and software components. They support heterogeneous communication protocols and bandwidth requirements.

Sensors: Capture physical signals from the environment. They provide raw data for perception, control, and analytics.

Processing_Units: Execute software algorithms and control logic. They provide computational resources for real-time and AI workloads.

V2X_Cloud_Comms: Enable communication with external vehicles, infrastructure, and cloud services. They support data sharing, remote updates, and cooperative functions.

Mobile_Platform_Actuators: Execute physical actions based on control commands. They include propulsion, steering, braking, and other motion-related mechanisms.
